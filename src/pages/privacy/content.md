# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 3 July 2026_

This policy explains how Lumphammer ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit this website. We are committed to handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## 1. Who We Are

Lumphammer is a software development business providing bespoke software for the tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) industry. For the purposes of UK data protection law, we are the data controller of personal data collected through this website.

Contact: [neil@lumphammer.com](mailto:neil@lumphammer.com)

## 2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

- **Contact data** — name and email address, when you contact us via the contact form
- **Usage data** — pages visited, browser type, device type, approximate location (country/region), and interaction data, collected via analytics

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 3. How We Collect Your Data

- Directly from you, when you fill in the contact form
- Automatically, via analytics software when you visit the website

## 4. Why We Process Your Data

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Responding to enquiries submitted via the contact form | Legitimate interests (to respond to people who contact us) |
| Understanding how the website is used to improve it | Legitimate interests (to maintain and improve our services) |

## 5. Analytics

This website uses [PostHog](https://posthog.com/) to collect anonymised usage analytics. PostHog may set cookies or use similar technologies to collect usage data. For details of how PostHog handles data, please refer to the [PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy).

## 6. How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Contact enquiries** — we retain messages and any associated personal data for up to 2 years, after which they are deleted.
- **Analytics data** — retained as configured in our PostHog account; typically no longer than 12 months.

## 7. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with the following third-party service providers who process it on our behalf:

- **PostHog** — analytics (see section 5)

We require all third parties to respect the security of your data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

## 8. Transfers Outside the UK

Some of our third-party service providers operate outside the UK. Where we transfer personal data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

## 9. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances
- **Restriction** — ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances
- **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [neil@lumphammer.com](mailto:neil@lumphammer.com). We will respond within one month.

## 10. Cookies

This website may use cookies for analytics purposes. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

## 11. How to Complain

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

- **Website:** [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk)
- **Helpline:** 0303 123 1113

We would appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns directly before you contact the ICO — please reach out to us first at [neil@lumphammer.com](mailto:neil@lumphammer.com).

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
